What is the problem?

Many people fail to see the environmental impact that comes with simply being careless in your home. For example, something as simple as accidentally leaving the lights on when you leave a room is actually wasting energy and putting your house at risk of fires.

There's much more than just this, however, that actively hurts the environment as a result of carelessness. Many, without knowing, regularly contribute to food waste, overuse of energy, and carbon emmissions, all from their own house. So, how can we stop this?




What we can do

There are many things you can do to help the environment from your home. These things include:

  1. Turning off the lights when you leave a room
  2. Limiting water usage during showers, sink using, etc.
  3. Using more natural energy (solar panels, amongst other things)
  4. Regularly checking in on friends and family to make sure they do the same

Doing these four simple things every day and being aware of the environment would be a huge step forward in keeping the Earth green!
